Astravon Live Arena

Intelligent Crowd Management, Computer Vision, AI Analytics and Emergency Response Platform developed by the House of Astravon.

Project Overview

Astravon Live Arena is an intelligent crowd monitoring and event safety platform that combines Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, Environmental Monitoring, Crowd Analytics, Risk Prediction and Emergency Coordination into a single real-time command center.

Designed for concerts, football stadiums, festivals, conferences, airports, transportation hubs and smart cities, the platform continuously analyses live video feeds and environmental conditions to improve public safety and operational awareness.

Mission

"Transform event safety through Artificial Intelligence, enabling organizers to predict, understand and respond to crowd behaviour before incidents occur."
